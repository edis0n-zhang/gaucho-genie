CANCELLATION OF REGISTRATION
Students who wish to cancel registration prior to the beginning of a quarter may do so on GOLD using the Quarter Cancellation Petition.

Note: International students and visiting students are not eligible to use the GOLD petition and will need to submit a signed Cancellation of Registration form to the Office of the Registrar.

Domestic students who are unable to use the GOLD Quarter Cancellation Petition will need to submit a signed Cancellation of Registration form to the Office of the Registrar.
